Curriculum Vitae



Bryan Robertson, a native of St. Louis, Missouri, draws inspiration from his family’s history, especially his grandparents’ escape from the Soviet takeover of Czechoslovakia and Poland. This experience instilled in him a deep appreciation for resilience, freedom, and the shared human experience, which he explores in his multimedia art. Passionate about visual arts, Bryan sees art as a universal language that connects people across time and place. His work has been exhibited nationally and internationally, earning recognition in various media. For Bryan, art is a bridge that unites people, telling stories that transcend borders and generations. Beyond his creative practice, Bryan has secured funding to support his research on the intersections of art, culture, and technology. Currently, he serves as the Dean of Visual and Performing Arts and 2D/3D Visual Art Faculty at Yavapai College in Arizona, inspiring others through his dedication to fostering creativity and connection.
