current work: 1984


My latest work, inspired by Orwell’s 1984, blends painting and digital media. Using looping animations, 3D models, and impasto paintings, I explore propaganda and truth manipulation. This Dada-influenced mix of digital collage and physicality reflects today’s fractured society and my evolving artistic voice.
